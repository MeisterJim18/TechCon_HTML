History

We are students from the Frankfurt University of Applied Sciences. In 2012, we witnessed the rise of smartphones with models like the iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, and Windows Phone. We started as a group of four tech enthusiasts, and soon realized many others shared our passion. Two years later, our group grew to 50 participants, then 100, and by 2023, we reached over 300 attendees.

Mission

Our goal is to inform and educate people about new technologies and the latest smartphones, showing how to use and optimize them as powerful allies in daily life.

Past Speakers

Among our first supporters was the President of Frankfurt UAS, who attended our initial meetings and guided our thematic development. The Communications Director of Deutsche Telekom helped connect us with leading smartphone and laptop brands. The Apple Germany Director has been a regular speaker, presenting new product launches each year.
